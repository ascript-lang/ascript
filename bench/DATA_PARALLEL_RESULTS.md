## (c) Frozen vs Plain Input — PMAP over shared.freeze vs per-chunk copy (W=4)

Frozen arrays cross the airlock with a single `Arc` bump per chunk (O(1) in N).
Plain arrays are deep-copied per chunk (O(N/chunks)).

**One-time freeze cost:**

|----------------|-----------|

| 100000 | 5.8 |

| 1000000 | 90.0 | 181.3 | 2.01× |

Frozen path confirms the §1 cost model: flat per-chunk crossing regardless of N.
